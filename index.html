Reservations can be made using an Android Phone with the ARIBO app downloaded, or at a tablet kiosk inside WTB

There is a Cushman Shuttle 6 wheelchair accessible vehicle option available. This comes with a ramp for your convenience

Autonomous

The shuttle is driverless and autonomously operated. A safety operator is on hand if you would like any assistance
